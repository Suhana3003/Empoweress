BASIC EXAMINATION FOR PCOS

THREE CRITERIA FOR BASIC LEVEL SCREENING:

AGE
BMI(Body Mass Index)
Menstrual Cycle(Regular/Irregular)




HOW TO CALCULATE BMI?

Weight(Kg)/Height(m2)

Significance of BMI...

STEP 1: Check your Menstrual Cycle whether it is regular or irregular.
STEP 2: Check your BMI.

CASE A: If according to your BMI, you come under over-weight criteria or obese criteria but your cycle is regular then your are less prone of having PCOS.

CASE B: If according to your BMI, you come under over-weight criteria or obese criteria and also, if your cycle is not regular then you have chances of having PCOS.

We suggest you go for some basic blood test and go for advance examination or visit a doctor according to your convenience.
